Whitespace workbench

Find where you're leaving value on the table versus competitors. The math runs here on your machine; the strategy judgment runs on your Claude subscription - no API key, nothing leaves your computer except what you paste into Claude yourself.

1Your data

Drop a file on a card (or click it to browse). Only brand_catalog.csv and competitor_catalog.csv and merchandising.yaml are required - everything else upgrades the analysis. Headers don't need to match exactly; common export names (Product, MSRP, SKU…) are understood.

2Run the checks

Deterministic math: validation, category mapping, composition, price comparables, capture gaps. Problems become flags, never silent fixes.

3The judgment step (your Claude does this)

Copy the reasoning prompt, paste it into claude.ai (any paid plan), then paste Claude's whole reply back below. The prompt carries the method, the output contracts, and your computed analysis - Claude writes the strategy; this page never calls a model.
